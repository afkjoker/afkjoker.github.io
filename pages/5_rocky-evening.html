Rocky Evening
Jul 4 2024
2:30 PM EST

I just heard that this evening, my family and I reserved a court at Carnegie Mellon for tennis. I haven't played tennis for a year, at most, and I don't know what calamities are going to occur this time. There was even one game where I was standing lazily on the sidelines with my back to the game, and a tennis ball shot right over the net and bonked the tennis racket right out of my hand!

I'm still not sure if I remember exactly how to play tennis. All I know is that you attempt to hit the ball with your racket, and hope it goes over the net and hope your opponent reflects it back to you. Serving has always been a problem for me, because I always miss it right when I drop it.

As you can tell, I'm definitely not the sporty type, but it's fun for exercise.

~ AFK Joker
