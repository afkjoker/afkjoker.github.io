Current Math Problems

I find several of the problems I do quite intriguing. For example, here's one!

This was basically a calculation of the area of the curve. Radii of the circles are each 1 unit, and each sectioned curve is $\frac{2\pi}{3}$




This question is pretty simple: just take note that the hexagon formed by the empty points is the center of the circle with three third-circles added. Since there are six third-circles outside the hexagon but inside the curve, add three third-circles to the hexagon's area to get the answer, $\boxed{(E) \pi + 6\sqrt{3}}$
